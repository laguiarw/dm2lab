About the DM² Lab

The DM² Lab is hosted at the Department of Business Administration of the University of Zurich, and is co-financed by the European Research Council (ERC) through the Consolidator Grant "Rethinking Copyright in the Digital Age: Balancing Incentives for Creation and Access" (RECODE, 2026-2031) and the Swiss National Science Foundation (SNSF) through the Project "Digitization and the Power of Online Platforms: Measurement and Implications" (2022-2026).

For any inquiries or feedback about the lab, please contact us.
